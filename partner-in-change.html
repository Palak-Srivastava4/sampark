Corporates

Thanks to a decade of dedicated investment by the Nayar Family, we've pioneered six innovations and established transformative five-year partnerships with 8 state governements. This foundational support has enabled us to impact over 100,000 schools and 1.5 Crore children. This remarkable journey, propelled by our Corporate Partners of Change, is driven by one mission: 'No Child Left Behind' – turning a bold vision into a tangible reality for millions.
