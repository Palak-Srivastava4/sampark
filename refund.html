Donation And Refund Policy

Sampark Foundation is instituting a donation refund policy to ensure fair and transparent processing of requests for refund of donations as digital payments are becoming more frequent. Sampark Foundation expects that all donors will exercise due care and diligence while making donations. Sampark Foundation also recognizes that a donation may be made erroneously, or donors may change their minds.

Sampark Foundation will examine each request for refund of donation and endeavor to make the refund. Sampark Foundation may also seek further information/documents and donors must co-operate in this regard.

However, Sampark Foundation is not obliged to make refunds and may, in its discretion, decline any requests for refund of donations, particularly if a tax exemption certificate has been issued.

If you would like your donation to Sampark Foundation to be refunded, you must request Sampark Foundation in writing or by email for a refund and Your request must reach Sampark Foundation within 15 (fifteen) days from the date on which you made the donation i.e. the date on which you:

Made the donation online, electronically, or through other means, OR

Handed over the cheque/demand draft to Sampark Foundation or someone authorized by Sampark Foundation for this purpose or despatched the cheque/demand draft to Sampark Foundation by other means. The written request stating the reason for requesting the refund must be sent to the address stated below and must contain all the following details pertaining to the donation:

  • Date of donation
  • Donation amount
  • If the donation was made through cheque/draft, please provide Cheque/Draft no.
  • If the donation was made through credit card, please provide Credit Card no. (last 4 digits only).
  • If the donation was made online, please provide Donation-ID.
