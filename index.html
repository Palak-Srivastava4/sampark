ENOUGH !!

If you are fortunate, there comes a time in your life when someone makes you realise that life's purpose can be much beyond the pursuit of profit and recognition. And so you start thinking of joy and happiness in terms of not consuming more but in making the lives of others as rewarding and as fulfilling as yours. My time to do that came in 2012, thanks to a nudge from my mother in the form of a letter with just one word, "Enough!". I quit my job and started my walk pursuing a new purpose.

Vineet Nayar

You Can’t Start A
Fire Without A Spark

Inspired by Bruce Springsteen, we call every SF team member a ‘Spark’ with
an aim to set young minds on fire by enabling them to learn better, faster
and greater. Our team of over 500 'sparks' works together in a symphony
using the GPS enabled Smart activity app along with Smart data analytics
engine, to drive program adoption at a granular level, one school at a time.

Our State Partners

Sampark Foundation was set up by Anupama Nayar and Vineet Nayar, former Vice Chairman and CEO of HCL Technologies and author of Harvard Business Press
bestseller "Employees First: Customer Second", with a belief that 'frugal innovation' and its effective implementation in partnership with the government can drive large-
scale improvement in learning outcomes. This thought prompted our founders to dedicate their lives to advancing the cause of primary education in the country. We
have already reached 10 million children in 1 lakh+ schools across 8 states.
